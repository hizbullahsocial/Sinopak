Premium Basmati Rice - Super Kernel

In Stock | Ready for Export

Super Kernel Basmati Rice is the premier variety of Pakistani Basmati, renowned worldwide for its extra-long grains, distinctive aroma, and exceptional taste. Grown in the fertile lands of Punjab, Pakistan, this rice is aged for a minimum of 6 months to enhance its flavor and cooking characteristics.

When cooked, the grains elongate to almost double their raw length, remain separate and fluffy, and release a delightful fragrance that elevates any culinary experience. Perfect for biryanis, pilafs, and as an accompaniment to curries and grilled dishes.

Key Features: 100% pure Super Kernel variety, minimum 6 months aging, high kernel length, aromatic, non-sticky after cooking, and sorted with color sorting machines.

Product Specifications

  • Variety: Super Kernel Basmati
  • Origin: Punjab, Pakistan
  • Grain Length: 6.8mm - 7.2mm (raw), 14mm+ (cooked)
  • Moisture Content: 13% max
  • Broken Grains: 1% max (Premium Grade)
  • Foreign Matter: 0.5% max
  • Aging: 6-12 months
  • Packaging Options: 1kg, 5kg, 10kg, 20kg, 50kg bags, or custom
  • Certifications: SGS Certified, Halal, ISO 22000
