About Our Company

Sino Pak Traders - Connecting Markets Worldwide

Founded in 2023, Sino Pak Traders specializes in the import and export of high-quality commodities, starting with Himalayan pink salt. We are committed to building sustainable trade relationships across continents.

Our mission is to source the finest products from around the world and deliver them to markets where they are most valued. We ensure ethical sourcing, quality control, and reliable logistics for every shipment.

While we currently focus on Himalayan pink salt, we are actively expanding our portfolio to include a wide range of premium spices, dried fruits, nuts, and other commodities.
