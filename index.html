About Our Company

Sino Pak Traders - Your Trusted Source for Premium Pakistani Products

Founded in 2023, Sino Pak Traders specializes in the export of high-quality Himalayan salt products and premium rice from Pakistan to global markets. We are committed to building sustainable trade relationships across continents.

Our product range includes finely ground and coarse Himalayan pink salt, hand-carved salt blocks for gourmet cooking, authentic salt lamps, and premium Basmati rice. We're also preparing to launch organic brown rice to meet growing health-conscious demand.

We ensure ethical sourcing from certified mines and farms, rigorous quality control at every stage, and reliable logistics for every shipment. Our team works closely with both suppliers and buyers to ensure complete satisfaction.
