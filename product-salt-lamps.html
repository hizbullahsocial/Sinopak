Himalayan Salt Lamps

In Stock | Ready to Ship

Our Himalayan Salt Lamps are hand-carved from natural salt crystals mined from the ancient Khewra Salt Mines in Pakistan. When illuminated from within by a small bulb, these lamps emit a warm, amber glow that creates a soothing, calming atmosphere in any space.

Beyond their aesthetic appeal, salt lamps are believed to offer natural air-purifying benefits. When warmed, the salt attracts water vapor from the air, trapping dust, pollen, and other pollutants. The heat then evaporates the water, releasing clean air while retaining the contaminants in the salt.

Key Features: 100% natural Himalayan salt crystal, hand-carved and hand-finished, includes electrical components (bulb, cord, switch), dimmer switch available on larger sizes, creates negative ions, unique natural shape and color variations.
