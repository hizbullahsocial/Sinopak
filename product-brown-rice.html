Organic Brown Rice

Coming Soon - Available for Pre-order

Our Organic Brown Rice is a nutritious, unpolished whole grain that retains its bran layer and germ, making it significantly more nutritious than white rice. Grown in the fertile regions of Pakistan using organic farming practices, this rice is free from synthetic pesticides and fertilizers.

Brown rice has a slightly nutty flavor and chewier texture compared to white rice. It's an excellent source of fiber, vitamins B1 and B3, magnesium, and iron. Perfect for health-conscious consumers, fitness enthusiasts, and anyone looking to incorporate more whole grains into their diet.

Key Features: 100% organic, unpolished, high fiber content, non-GMO, chemical-free farming, rich in nutrients.

Product Specifications

  • Variety: Organic Brown Rice (Long Grain)
  • Origin: Punjab, Pakistan
  • Cultivation: Certified Organic (no pesticides, no chemicals)
  • Grain Length: 6.2mm - 6.8mm
  • Moisture Content: 14% max
  • Broken Grains: 2% max
  • Foreign Matter: 0.5% max
  • Packaging Options: 1kg, 5kg, 10kg, 20kg, 50kg bags, or custom
  • Certifications: USDA Organic, EU Organic (in process), ISO 22000
