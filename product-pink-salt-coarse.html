Himalayan Pink Salt - Coarse Grain

In Stock | Ready to Ship

Our coarse grain Himalayan Pink Salt features larger, crystalline granules that are perfect for salt grinders, gourmet cooking, and bath products. Mined from the ancient Khewra Salt Mines in Pakistan, these crystals retain their beautiful pink hue and mineral-rich composition.

The coarse texture provides a satisfying crunch when used as a finishing salt on meats, vegetables, and salads. It's also ideal for filling salt mills, allowing you to grind fresh salt directly onto your dishes. Beyond culinary uses, these crystals are popular for bath soaks and spa treatments due to their high mineral content.

Key Features: 100% natural crystals, no additives, rich in 84+ trace minerals, versatile for cooking and wellness, sustainably harvested.

Product Specifications

  • Origin: Khewra Salt Mines, Punjab, Pakistan
  • Purity: 98-99% Sodium Chloride with natural trace minerals
  • Particle Size: 2mm - 5mm (Coarse/Crystal)
  • Color: Natural pink to deep orange-pink
  • Moisture Content: < 0.5%
  • Insoluble Matter: < 0.5%
  • Packaging Options: 500g, 1kg, 5kg, 10kg, 25kg bags, or custom
  • Shelf Life: Indefinite when stored in dry conditions
  • Certifications: ISO 22000, HACCP, Halal Certified
