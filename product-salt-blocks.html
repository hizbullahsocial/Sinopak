Himalayan Salt Blocks

In Stock | Ready to Ship

Our Himalayan Salt Blocks are hand-carved from massive crystals of ancient sea salt, mined from the Khewra Salt Mines in Pakistan. These versatile blocks serve multiple purposes: as gourmet cooking surfaces, elegant serving platters, and even as therapeutic tools.

When heated, salt blocks provide a natural, non-stick surface for searing meats, vegetables, and seafood, imparting a delicate salt flavor. When chilled, they become perfect for serving cheeses, sushi, desserts, and cold appetizers. The natural pink and orange hues create stunning presentations.

Key Features: Hand-carved from single crystals, naturally anti-microbial, retains heat/cold exceptionally well, imparts subtle mineral flavor, reusable with proper care, each block is unique.
